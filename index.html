Maputo, Mozambique

Maputo is the vibrant capital of Mozambique, located on the southeastern coast of Africa. Known for its rich history, colonial architecture, and bustling markets, it's a cultural and economic hub. The city offers a mix of modern development and historical landmarks, such as the Iron House and the Maputo Railway Station. Its coastal location provides beautiful views of the Indian Ocean, making it a lively and scenic destination.
